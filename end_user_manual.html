User Manual

A practical guide for Guest Demo, predictions, playoffs picks, Today's Edge, Super Swapper, My Team, Frozen Squads, Leaderboard, Rules, and Profile.

Login Guest Demo Predictions Playoffs Today's Edge My Team Super Swapper Frozen Squads Leaderboard Profile

1. Login / Register

  1. Open the IFL app URL.
  2. Login with phone number and password.
  3. New users register with team name, phone number, and password.
  4. Your phone number must be allowed by admin.

Guest Demo

  • Use Guest Demo from the public home page to explore IFL without joining.
  • Guest Demo uses dummy data only and does not save predictions, swaps, or team details.
  • It includes simulated Home, Predictions, Today's Edge, My Team, Super Swapper, Playoffs, Rules, and Profile screens.
  • Guest Home includes a demo leader highlighter and demo leaderboard movement.

2. Predictions

  1. Open Predictions.
  2. Select your winner for each unlocked match.
  3. Edit before the prediction lock time.
  4. Correct winner gives 50 points.
If admin marks a match as no result, prediction points are 0 for everyone.

3. Playoffs Prediction

  1. Open Playoffs Prediction.
  2. Pick 4 likely playoff teams.
  3. Save while the window is open.
  4. Use reset to clear and repick before the window closes.

4. Today's Edge

  • See daily match and leaderboard context.
  • Review what can change today.
  • Use Galgotias Insight Box for button-based insights.
  • Check rank gaps, active players, unique/differential indicators, and leverage snapshot.

5. My Team

  • View your current effective squad.
  • Player cards show archetype avatars such as Run Machine, Wicket Hunter, Chaos Pick, Clutch Finisher, and Dark Horse.
  • Hover on desktop or tap on mobile to flip cards and view accumulated player points.
  • On mobile, flipped cards automatically return after 3 seconds.
  • Check match-wise player points and prediction contribution.
  • Review swapped player frozen points after swaps become effective.

6. Super Swapper

  1. Select 1 to 3 players from current squad as swap out.
  2. Select the same number from player pool as swap in.
  3. Use search, team, role, Indian/International filters, and point sorting.
  4. Check validation summary.
  5. Click Submit Swaps, then Freeze Swaps when final.
Final squad must stay at 20 players, minimum 6 bowlers, maximum 8 international players.

7. Frozen Squads

  • View other available user squads.
  • Squads reflect latest effective swaps.

8. Leaderboard

  • Track rank, last earned points, total points, and prediction points.
  • Rank arrows show movement versus previous snapshot.
  • Gap insights highlight major differences between adjacent ranks.
  • The logged-in Home page also shows a current leader spotlight with a rotating pedestal and leader logo.
  • The Home leaderboard preview auto-scrolls when enough ranks are available.

9. Rules

  • Open Rules for squad, prediction, scoring, and swap rules.
  • Runout and stumping use the same points bucket.

10. Profile

  • View account and team summary.
  • Upload a team logo in PNG, JPG, or WEBP format.
  • Use a square image under 2 MB for best results.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Cannot login: confirm your phone number is allowed and password is correct.
  • Cannot predict: the match prediction window may be locked.
  • Cannot submit swaps: check active window, number of swaps, bowler count, and international count.
  • Swap not visible in current squad: the effective match may not have been reached yet.
  • Points not updated: wait until admin publishes scoring.
  • Guest Demo changes not saved: this is expected because Guest Demo is read-only.

Tip Super Swapper changes are reflected through effective squad logic, not by editing your original squad.
