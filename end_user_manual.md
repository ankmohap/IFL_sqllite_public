# IFL 2026 User Manual

This guide is for IFL users.

## 1. Login / Register
1. Open the IFL app URL.
2. Login with your phone number and password.
3. If you are a new user, register with team name, phone number, and password.
4. Your phone number must already be allowed by admin.

### Guest Demo
Use **Guest Demo** from the public home page if you want to explore IFL without joining the league.

Guest Demo:
- uses dummy data only
- does not create your team
- does not save predictions or swaps
- shows a sample Home, Predictions, Today's Edge, My Team, Super Swapper, Playoffs, Rules, and Profile flow

## 2. Predictions
1. Open **Predictions**.
2. Select the team you think will win.
3. You can update a prediction until the prediction lock time.
4. Correct winner prediction gives 50 points.
5. If admin marks the match as no result, prediction points are 0 for everyone.

## 3. Playoffs Prediction
1. Open **Playoffs Prediction**.
2. Pick 4 teams you think will qualify for playoffs.
3. Save your selection while the window is open.
4. Use reset if you want to clear and repick before the window closes.

## 4. Today's Edge
Today's Edge gives daily league context.

You can see:
- current match impact
- leaderboard gaps
- What Can Change Today
- Galgotias Insight Box

Galgotias Insight Box is button based. Click an insight button and the app shows calculated fantasy insights from current IFL data.

Today's Edge also shows:
- your current rank and gaps around you
- active players for the current match
- unique/differential player indicators
- leverage snapshot
- What Can Change Today scenarios

## 5. My Team
Open **My Team** to see:
- your current effective squad
- player cards grouped by role
- player archetype avatars such as Run Machine, Wicket Hunter, Chaos Pick, Clutch Finisher, Dark Horse, and more
- popularity stars based on ownership
- player points on card flip
- match-wise points
- prediction contribution
- swapped player frozen points

On desktop, hover over a player card to flip it.

On mobile, tap a player card to flip it. The card automatically flips back after 3 seconds.

After a swap becomes effective for a match window, swapped-in players appear in your current squad and swapped-out players are shown separately with their frozen points.

## 6. Super Swapper
Open **Super Swapper** during an active swap window.

The screen has three main sections:
- current squad for swap out
- swap box showing selected out/in cards
- player pool for swap in

How to use:
1. Select 1 to 3 players from your current squad as swap out players.
2. Select the same number of players from the player pool as swap in players.
3. Use search and filters to find players by name, team, role, or Indian/International status.
4. Use point sorting to compare high and low scoring players.
5. Check the validation summary.
6. Click **Submit Swaps** to save your selected swaps.
7. Click **Freeze Swaps** when you want to lock your saved swaps.
8. Use **Reset Saved Swaps** if you want to clear and choose again while allowed.

Validation rules:
- minimum 1 swap and maximum 3 swaps
- resulting squad must remain exactly 20 players
- resulting squad must have minimum 6 bowlers
- resulting squad must have maximum 8 international players

## 7. Frozen Squads
Open **Frozen Squads** to view available squads for other users.

This screen shows latest effective squads, so swaps are reflected after their effective match point.

## 8. Leaderboard
Open **Leaderboard** to see:
- current rank
- team name
- last earned points
- total points
- prediction points
- rank movement arrows
- important rank gaps

Rank movement is compared against stored leaderboard snapshots from the previous day.

On the logged-in Home page, a preview leaderboard auto-scrolls when enough teams are available. The Home page also highlights the current leader with a rotating disc/pedestal and the leader's team logo.

## 9. Rules
Open **Rules** to review:
- squad rules
- prediction rules
- scoring rules
- swap rules

## 10. Profile
Open **Profile** to view your account/team summary and upload your team logo.

Logo guidance:
- PNG, JPG, or WEBP
- recommended square image
- maximum 2 MB

## Quick Troubleshooting
- Cannot login: confirm your phone number is allowed and password is correct.
- Cannot predict: the match prediction window may be locked.
- Cannot submit swaps: check active window, number of swaps, bowler count, and international count.
- Swap not visible in current squad: the swap may not be frozen/validated yet or its effective match id has not been reached.
- Points not updated: wait until admin publishes scoring for the match.
- Guest Demo changes not saved: this is expected because Guest Demo is read-only.
